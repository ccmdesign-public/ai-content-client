## [Towards GenAI](https://medium.com/towards-genai) General Rules

Everyone is welcome to join and write for [**Towards GenAI**](https://medium.com/towards-genai) we believe that everyone has valuable blogs/guide/stories to share.

4 rules you need to keep in mind before submitting your story.

-   **There are no topic restrictions.**
-   **We take drafts and self-published stories.**
-   **You can submit as many stories as you like.**
-   **Your story must provide AI or Technology value to readers.**

You might think the 4th step is too vague. If I define it more deeply.

The pub is new and I encourage everyone to submit stories, but the main aim here is to build a community that provides readers some value.

Your stories can help them improve their life, work, habits, careers, minds, knowledge, ideas, chores, and the list goes on.

> ***From daily chores to practical applications to entrepreneurial journeys. Your experience and knowledge in any A.I. are welcome here.***

## Blogs/Guide/Stories requirements

**Length:
**Your story should be above 200 and below 20,000 words.

**AI-generated content:
**Welcome to some extent here. In the case of full AI content detected, stories will be rejected. So kindly think before you submit.

**Plagiarism:
**Not welcome in this pub. Kindly make sure to follow the Medium rules. References & citations should also be at end of the articles

**Pictures:
**Make sure to include an image source in case you have not created it yourself. You can use many websites like Unsplash, Pexels, etc.

If using AI generated image, please mention “AI generated Image by author”

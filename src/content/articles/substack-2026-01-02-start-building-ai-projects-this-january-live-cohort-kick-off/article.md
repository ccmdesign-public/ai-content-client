# Start Building AI Projects This January - Live Cohort Kick-Off This Sunday

The hardest part of learning AI isn’t effort.
It’s turning curiosity into **consistent, hands-on progress**.

That’s what our monthly cohort kick-off is designed to solve.

This Sunday, the **January cohort** kicks off with a live session led by our CEO, Louie Peters. The session walks through how learners actually use Towards AI to move forward: how they choose a path, what to work on first, and how people go from concepts to **real projects** instead of staying stuck in tutorials.

If you joined recently, this is your on-ramp.
If you’re still exploring and deciding whether to join, you’re welcome to attend and see how the learning paths work before committing.

By the end of January, some learners will be:

-   building AI apps in Python instead of just reading about them
    
-   working with prompting, RAG, agents, and evaluation in structured projects
    
-   shipping systems they can explain, demo, and build on
    

Others will still be bookmarking posts and waiting for the “right time.”

The cohort kick-off happens **once a month**, and January’s session is this Sunday.

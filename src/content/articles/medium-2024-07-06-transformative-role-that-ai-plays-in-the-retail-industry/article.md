AI in retail plays a key role in deriving key insights with regard to various markets for enhancing different touchpoints in the customer service sector. It is useful in addressing issues pertaining to fit and fabric in the [artificial intelligence in apparel industry](https://www.anolytics.ai/solutions/retail/). It will play a key role in ensuring that our garments are tailored as per our body shape in fit and size.

AI offers the customer with a 3D sizing, which is an eight digit number which defines each individual’s shape. This number is used to define shape by nationality, age and race. A combination of data and technology aids in producing a sizing scale for a consumer of any age, race or at any place. It also provides patterns by computer in real time at a minimal cost.

So, it will be worthwhile to shift our focus now to how AI in retail will play a key role in transforming the entire retail industry.

1.  **Inventory Management:** Enhanced demand forecasting is the outcome of leveraging AI in retail. By mining insights from marketplace, consumer and competitor data, AI business intelligence tools forecast industry shifts in order to make proactive changes to the company’s marketing, merchandising, and business strategies. It also impacts the supply chain planning along with pricing and promotional planning.
2.  **Adaptive Homepage:** A customer’s current context, prior purchases, shopping history is being recognized and customized by mobile and digital portals to reveal the customer’s current scenario, previous scenario and shopping behavior. AI systems are constantly evolving to enhance a user’s digital experience and creating hyper-relevant displays for every interaction.
3.  **Dynamic Outreach:** A detailed shopper profile is developed through repeated customer interactions for acquiring a know-how of customer’s tastes and preferences via CRM and marketing systems. The informtion is then used to deliver proactive and personalized outbound marketing customized recommendations, rewards, or content.
4.  **Interactive Chat:** AI chatbots play an indispensible role in the retail industry by enhancing customer service and engagement. Chatbots use AI and machine learning to communicate with customers with the goal of answering common queries and directing the customers with helpful answers and results.
5.  **Visual Curation:** Browsing patterns of customers are translated into digital retail opportunities using algorithmic engines. This allows customers to look for new products using image-based search and analysis to create recommendations on the basis of aesthetics and similarity.
6.  **Guided Discovery:** As customers look forward to establishing confidence with regard to purchase decisions, automated assistants help in narrowing down choice by suggesting products based on shoppers’ requirements, choices and suitability.
7.  **Conversational Support:** AI assisted conversational assistants use natural language processing to assist shoppers in navigating questions, FAQs with minimal effort for redirecting to a human expert as and when needed. This enhances customer experience through on-demand, readily available support and streamlines staffing.
8.  **Personalization & Customer Insights:** Custom shopping experience is created for every visitor using biometric recognition. This assists with customer profiles, loyalty accounts, unlocked rewards and promotions. Stores also make use of AI and advanced algorithms to comprehend customers’ interests on the basis of demographic data, social media behavior, and purchase patterns. This data further assists in enhancing the shopping experience and personalized service (online as well as offline).
9.  **Emotional Response:** AI interfaces can recognize shopper’s emotions, reactions, etc. by interpreting facial, biometric and audio cues for proper retail engagement.
10.  **Customer Engagement:** Retailers use IoT enabled technologies for interacting with customers to gain insights regarding customer behavior preferences without having to interact with them.

***Summary****: ArtificiaI intelligence (AI) is a driving force in the* [*retail sector*](https://www.anolytics.ai/blog/retail-processes-are-being-redefined-by-artificial-intelligence/) *as it makes it very convenient for retailers to keep a tab on customer demand, preferences, budget, etc. This plays a key role in boosting sales and customer retention.*

## Important AI reality check.

-   **AI ≠ Product
    **The experience is. That’s why we need AI Product Managers.
    
-   **AI Agents ≠ Chatbots
    **AI gents are way more than just sugarcoated chatbots. Why? Because they LEARN, they become better and then they become proactive.
    
-   **AI Product Management ≠ AI \*for\* Product Management
    **I can’t stress this enough. AI Product Management means literally working with scientists and building the APIs, tools, models, vs AI \*for\* Product Management which is more about becoming AI-enhanced by leveraging GenAI tools for your *own* impact. More on this [here](https://marily.substack.com/p/what-kind-of-ai-pm-will-you-be-in).
    
-   **AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) ≠ Just a bigger, better AI
    **I attended a talk by Sam Altman and he mentioned that AGI is actually a debate. This point really stuck with me. An AGI test, is actually designed to evaluate an AI system's ability to learn and generalize from *limited* information so AGI does not mean just a bigger AI. Here is [how to assess an LLM](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/marilynika_%F0%9D%97%A7%F0%9D%97%B5%F0%9D%97%B2-%F0%9D%97%A5%F0%9D%97%B6%F0%9D%97%B4%F0%9D%97%B5%F0%9D%98%81-%F0%9D%97%AA%F0%9D%97%AE%F0%9D%98%86-%F0%9D%98%81%F0%9D%97%BC-%F0%9D%97%96%F0%9D%97%BC%F0%9D%97%BA%F0%9D%97%BD%F0%9D%97%AE%F0%9D%97%BF%F0%9D%97%B2-activity-7290468239212482562-1Dcr?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAAfEfpIBWJvpL2hyDqEbLsuOJilNUhJsia8).
    
-   **AI ethics ≠ a solved problem
    **Unfortunately to this day, builders don’t have ethic in the back of their head as they are launching products. This is still by NO means solved, and we are all responsible here.
    
-   **your skills + AI ≠ your skills OR AI
    **This is getting old, but…. you are not going to get replaced by AI. But people that have this “+AI” next to their skillset, will, very much so, get replaced by those that do.
    
-   **AI in Healthcare ≠ Just diagnostic tools
    **Healthcare is probably the domain I’m most excited about when it comes to AI’s potential. From drug discovery to personalized medicine and virtual care, there is just so much that is unexplored. AI is definitely just a passive diagnostic tool when it comes to AI.

## Key Takeaways

Ahrefs' latest product update focuses on expanding brand monitoring and improving user experience across its SEO and analytics platform.

## Summary

The December 2025 Ahrefs product update introduces nine significant enhancements, with the spotlight on expanding **Brand Radar**'s monitoring capabilities.

**Brand Monitoring Expands to TikTok and Reddit**
Brand Radar now indexes **TikTok** videos, scanning descriptions and transcripts to surface brand mentions in popular content. This joins existing YouTube tracking, with both platforms in beta for all paid customers before moving to a $199/month add-on. A new **Reddit insert index** tracks brand visibility across Reddit results ranking in Google Search, analyzing titles, descriptions, and subreddit names in SERP snippets. Reddit tracking is also free during beta.

**Custom AI Query Tracking**
A major addition allows users to track brand mentions for their own **custom prompts** across AI assistants. This feature shows how AI assistants like **ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Copilot** talk about a brand for specific buyer questions. Users can attach saved reports to projects, set tracking frequencies (daily, weekly, monthly), and use checks based on prompt, model, and location. Brand Radar now runs on two data sets: the global Ahrefs data set and project-level custom queries, with a new filter to switch between them.

**UX Improvements Across the Platform**
- **Brand Radar Interface:** Long AI responses are now collapsed by default with details in a side panel. New columns show mentioned brands, and favicons for cited websites help with pattern spotting.

- **Quick Filters:** A new 'Your Brand' filter lets users include or exclude their own brand from AI visibility reports with one click.

- **Web Analytics:** Users can now import historical **Google Analytics** data to analyze trends without losing context when native tracking begins.

- **Report Builder:** Revamped Brand Radar widgets can now chart additional metrics like citations, impressions, and AI share of voice.

- **Site Explorer:** The intent history graph now includes organic traffic by intent. A new AI-powered 'page type' column in SERP overviews classifies content (e.g., articles, landing pages, tools).

- **Social Media Manager:** Engagement metrics (views, reactions) are now visible within the manager, with historical backfilling for a complete performance baseline.

**API Enhancements**
New **Brand Radar API endpoints** provide data on mentions distribution across AI clients and time series for mentions/impressions. The **Site Audit Projects API** upgrade allows pulling historical crawl data, querying individual projects, and accessing richer metrics like URL counts and error details.

## Context

This update reflects the growing importance of monitoring brand presence across emerging digital channels beyond traditional search. As AI-generated content and social platforms like TikTok and Reddit increasingly influence brand perception and search visibility, SEO and marketing professionals need tools to track mentions comprehensively. These enhancements help businesses understand their visibility across the fragmented digital landscape where consumers discover brands.

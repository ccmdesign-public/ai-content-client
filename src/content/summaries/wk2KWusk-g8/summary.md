## Key Takeaways

Ahrefs has released several new features and updates in March 2025 to enhance SEO and marketing tools. • **Brand Radar** tracks brand mentions in AI search to optimize visibility and stay competitive. • **Web Analytics** now offers enhanced visitor trend charts with customizable metrics and manual item selection. • **Site Explorer** identifies paid keywords with organic top-10 rankings to align PPC and SEO strategies. • **Ahrefs Certification** provides a 67-question exam to validate skills and boost career opportunities.

## Summary

Ahrefs has introduced a suite of updates in March 2025, starting with the new **Brand Radar** tool. Brand Radar allows users to track their brand's visibility in AI search by analyzing terms, suggesting competitors, and comparing brand mentions in LLMs. It includes filters to see exact search queries and AI citations where competitors are featured, helping optimize for the AI game.

**Web Analytics** has been improved for easier exploration of visitor trends. Clicking 'view more' on any widget now displays a chart of the top five results by visitors over time, with the ability to switch between key metrics like bounce rate and visit duration. Users can manually select up to 100 items to track trends and compare performance more effectively.

In **Site Explorer**, the general and page search tabs now show paid keywords for which the target already ranks organically in the top 10. This feature helps align PPC and SEO strategies by avoiding bidding on keywords with strong organic visibility, making every ad dollar count.

The **SERP overview** inside Explorer now automatically matches reports comparison mode, whereas it previously opened in non-comparative analysis, streamlining workflow.

**Keywords Explorer** has added custom filter presets that users can set, save, and star for instant access. These presets are stored per user and are not shared between users in a workspace, allowing for personalized and efficient keyword analysis.

**Rank Tracker** updates include sparkline charts for GSC keywords and GSC pages reports, enabling users to instantly spot trends for clicks and impressions without extra digging. The tag filter has been upgraded to allow multiple rules and conditions, similar to keyword and URL filters, providing more flexibility to segment data and track performance precisely.

Finally, Ahrefs has launched its **official certification** exam. The exam takes 1 hour and consists of 67 questions, requiring a score of 75% or higher to earn a sharable certificate valid for one year. This certification aims to help professionals learn better, attract bigger clients, and build lasting marketing success, with incentives like sharing on social media for a chance to win swag.

## Context

Ahrefs is a leading SEO tool suite used by digital marketers and SEO professionals to optimize search performance. These updates are critical as AI-driven search transforms how brands are discovered, requiring advanced tracking and analysis tools to stay competitive. The new certification helps professionals validate their skills in a rapidly evolving market, enhancing career prospects and client trust.

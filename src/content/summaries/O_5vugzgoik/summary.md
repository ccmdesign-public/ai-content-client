## Key Takeaways

The experiment shows AI agents can run autonomously with tangible results. Key findings include: • **Steady growth** across platforms with 918K X impressions and 322 YouTube subscribers • **Profitability achieved** with $141 revenue covering operational costs • **Multi-platform automation** using skills for X, YouTube, and store promotion • **Future potential** demonstrated through job application experiments landing interviews

## Summary

The creator ran an AI agent autonomously for 504 hours (3 weeks) on a Mac Mini to test long-term autonomous operation. The agent was built using a custom solution similar to nano claw, running on cloud code with a loop connected to WhatsApp for monitoring.

The agent had multiple skills including react videos for YouTube, X coding, promotional videos, video research, Gmail, GitHub, LinkedIn, LLM bench, and Poly market integration. These skills allowed it to operate across platforms without constant human supervision.

### Platform Performance Results
**X (Twitter) Analytics:**
• 918,000 impressions over 3 weeks
• 852 followers gained (approximately 40 per day)
• 4,000 likes and 28,000 engagements
• 15,000 profile visits

**YouTube Performance:**
• 322 subscribers gained
• 28,000 views
• 700 watchtime hours
• Automatic posting and cross-promotion to X

**Revenue Generation:**
The agent ran the Skills MD store, promoting its own setup tools including Chrome automation CLI for agents.
• 41 sales made through Stripe
• $141 total revenue ($2.99 and $4.99 pricing tiers)
• Operational costs approximately $100-120/month
• Net profit of about $20, making the experiment cost-neutral

### Technical Setup and Future PlansThe agent runs on a Mac Mini with low electricity consumption. The creator mentioned having a separate private system for job applications that has landed actual interviews. Future plans include:
• Adding streaming capabilities to show the agent's activities in real-time
• Training new skills
• Creating more content about using AI for YouTube video creation
• Exploring Poly market integration further

The experiment demonstrates that while not revolutionary in scale, AI agents can maintain steady growth and even generate revenue when given specific tasks and skills.

## Context

This experiment matters because it demonstrates the practical viability of autonomous AI agents in real-world scenarios. As AI systems become more capable, understanding how they can operate independently with minimal human oversight is crucial for developers, entrepreneurs, and businesses looking to automate content creation, marketing, and revenue generation. The video shows that even simple AI agents can produce measurable results across multiple platforms simultaneously, suggesting a future where AI assistants handle routine digital tasks while humans focus on higher-level strategy and creativity.

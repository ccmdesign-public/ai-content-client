A major differentiator is its aggressive pricing. At a blended rate of $0.50 per million tokens for input and output, it is presented as 10 to 20 times cheaper than many competitors. **Broader Implications**
The self-evolution workflow represents a forward-looking approach to model development that is predicted to become more common. Combined with its cost, strong performance on coding tasks, and agentic proficiency, M2.7 is positioned as a highly practical and compelling option for software engineering and real-world task automation.

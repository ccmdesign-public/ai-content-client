## Summary

The Claude Ads skill for Claude Code is a comprehensive paid advertising audit and optimization tool that replaces traditional ad agencies. It covers six major advertising platforms with over 190 audit checks, industry-specific templates, and parallel sub-agent delegation for efficient processing.

**Installation and Setup** is straightforward through terminal commands, making it accessible for users across different IDEs like Cursor, Anti-gravity, Warp, or directly in the terminal. Once installed, users launch Claude Code and activate the skill with a simple `/ads` command to begin the advertising planning process.

**Interactive Strategy Development** begins with targeted questions about business type (SaaS, e-commerce, local, B2B), advertising goals (brand awareness, conversions), budget range, and current ad status. The agent then analyzes the business website when provided, extracting key information about the product and target audience to inform strategy.

**Platform Selection and Budget Allocation** is data-driven based on the agent's knowledge base of 2,500+ websites about best advertising practices. For example, with a $1,000 monthly budget focused on brand awareness, the agent recommended Meta (Facebook and Instagram) exclusively, noting that LinkedIn is too expensive and Google Search is conversion-focused rather than awareness-oriented.

**Comprehensive Output Generation** includes multiple deliverables: competitive landscape analysis, platform rationale, budget allocation plans, creative briefs, and expected results metrics. The agent creates professional PDF reports with charts and styling, automatically fixing formatting issues and ensuring polished final documents.

**Customization and Integration** capabilities allow users to request specific platforms (YouTube-only, Google Ads, etc.), different output formats, or connections to external services via MCP servers for Google Analytics integration or API-based content generation.

## Key Takeaways

The video presents a three-phase AI prompting system to quickly become competent in unfamiliar situations. • **Map phase**: Use AI to learn key vocabulary, concepts, and normal processes in a new domain. • **Mine phase**: Apply **inversion thinking** to identify hidden risks, expensive mistakes, and second-order effects. • **Move phase**: Create actionable cheat sheets with critical questions, next steps, and red flags to watch for.

## Summary

The video introduces a structured three-phase system called **Map, Mine, Move** that transforms how professionals use AI to tackle unfamiliar challenges. This method turns AI from a basic question-answering tool into a strategic thinking partner that helps avoid costly blind spots.

### The Three-Phase System

**Map Phase** starts with a specific prompt template where you declare your role, situation, and lack of expertise. You ask the AI for a fifth-grade-level executive briefing on the domain, key concepts and terms, major approaches, normal processes, and what outsiders consistently get wrong. This builds foundational knowledge quickly.

**Mine Phase** applies the **inversion principle** by asking what could go wrong. Using dictation for richer context, you prompt the AI to uncover hidden complexities, expensive mistakes newcomers make, second-order effects, and insider wisdom a 20-year veteran would share. This phase reveals pitfalls you wouldn't discover until it's too late.

**Move Phase** turns insights into action. You ask for the 3-5 most important questions to ask, specific information to gather, the smartest first step to take immediately, and red flags to watch for. This creates a practical cheat sheet for your actual conversation or project.

### Critical Implementation DetailsThree rules ensure success: **stay in the same conversation** to compound context across phases, **use top-tier reasoning models** (GPT-5.2 with extended reasoning, Claude Opus 4.6, or Gemini 3 Pro), and **use dictation** when describing your situation to provide richer, unedited context.

The system demonstrates how AI enables professionals to become "jacks of all trades" who can leverage AI for depth while maintaining breadth across multiple domains.

## Context

Professionals constantly face situations outside their expertise—hiring for unfamiliar roles, evaluating unknown software, or handling legal matters. Traditional approaches leave dangerous knowledge gaps, while basic AI queries yield average results. This system matters because it transforms AI from an information tool into a strategic thinking partner that helps identify blind spots before they become costly mistakes. It's particularly valuable for business owners, leaders, and employees who need to quickly become competent in new domains without years of specialization.

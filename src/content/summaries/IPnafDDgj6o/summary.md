## Key Takeaways

The video reveals a legal system for generating significant monthly income on Facebook using other people's content as inspiration. • **Facebook pays creators** due to a content supply-demand imbalance, unlike Instagram. • **Add transformative value** via commentary/insight to qualify for fair use, avoiding simple reaction videos. • **Native uploads and niche consistency** are critical for maximizing reach and monetization rates. • **Convert platform traffic to email subscribers** using tools like Beehive or Spark Loop for sustainable audience ownership.

## Summary

Chris Koerner discloses a method generating $10,000-$35,000 monthly profit on Facebook by legally repurposing content from other creators, spending only about 3 hours per week. The opportunity exists because Facebook has 2.1 billion daily active users but a shortage of quality creators, so it pays to fill the content gap.

**The core strategy involves finding proven viral content** on Instagram Reels or Twitter within your niche, then adding substantial commentary or insight. This qualifies as **fair use**—you must transform the original by providing analysis, critique, or unique perspective. Simple reaction videos without added value are not protected. Koerner demonstrates by taking a viral reel about a junk removal business and overlaying his own video critique, explaining how he would improve the business model.

**Critical tactical steps include:**
- **Never cross-post**; upload natively to each platform (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, etc.) for maximum reach.

- **Start a fresh Instagram account** for research, using it to discover viral content in your niche.

- **Edit efficiently** using CapCut or Instagram's Edits app, focusing on removing dead air and adding captions/overlays.

- **Attribute original creators** by tagging them when possible, though not legally required.

- **Take a hard stance** in your commentary; nuanced takes don't perform as well.

**Monetization occurs through Facebook's invite-only program**, which pays based on views and niche (business/finance niches earn ~$0.25 per 1,000 views). Consistency and fair use compliance lead to an invitation. However, the **real long-term value is converting traffic to email subscribers**. Koerner uses a link in every post to a landing page (built with Beehive or Card) and Spark Loop to earn additional revenue per email captured, building an owned audience insulated from platform algorithm changes.

**AI tools like Gemini** can help generate commentary ideas or scripts if you struggle with inspiration. The process is repeatable and scalable, with Koerner showing examples where 5-10 minute videos (sometimes edited for $10) earned $500-$2,000 each.

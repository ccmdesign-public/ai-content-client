## Key Takeaways

Granola is an AI note-taking tool that combines manual note-taking with automated transcription and enhancement to capture meeting context effectively.

- **Preload questions** before meetings to make them 10x more productive by giving the AI your agenda.

- **Don't write perfect notes**—just tag key moments (decisions, action items, risks) and let Granola's transcript fill in the context.

- **Build custom recipes** for repeated meeting types (standups, design reviews) and use **workspaces grouped by project** so the AI has full context.

## Summary

Granola addresses the common problem for product designers who spend significant time in meetings but struggle to capture all the important context, feedback, and decisions. Traditional note-taking methods often miss crucial details, while pure transcription apps lack the personal notes that highlight what matters most.

Granola solves this by functioning like **Apple Notes for meeting capture**—you write bullet points manually while it automatically transcribes the conversation in the background. After the meeting, it **enhances your handwritten notes** based on the full transcript, filling in gaps and providing richer context.

Key features include:

- **Interactive chat with transcripts**: Ask questions like "list my to-dos" or use specific commands to extract information

- **Pre-built and custom recipes**: Templates for different meeting types (client kickoffs, one-on-ones, design critiques) that structure your notes

- **Calendar integration**: Automatically pulls in meeting details and allows pre-meeting agenda setting

Margerie demonstrates a practical workflow where he uses Granola to capture a Discord community meeting, then exports the enhanced notes as Markdown and feeds them into **Claude Code** with a prompt to build a landing page for his Discord server. The AI generates a responsive page with quotes from the transcript and meeting summaries.

### Practical Tips for Implementation

- **Spend 30 seconds preloading questions** before important meetings to structure the conversation

- **Focus on tagging rather than perfect notes**—use keywords that will serve as anchors in the transcript

- **Create recipes for repetitive meetings** and refine them over time for consistency

- **Organize by project workspaces** rather than meeting type so the AI understands the full project context

This approach transforms meeting notes from passive records into active assets that can be directly used for prototyping, documentation, and project execution.

## Context

For product designers and engineers who spend significant time in meetings, capturing actionable insights and maintaining project context is challenging. Traditional note-taking misses nuance, while pure transcription lacks prioritization. This matters because meetings are where critical design decisions, feedback, and project direction are established—losing that context leads to rework and misalignment. Granola represents the convergence of AI-assisted productivity tools that help knowledge workers focus on conversation rather than documentation while ensuring nothing valuable is lost.

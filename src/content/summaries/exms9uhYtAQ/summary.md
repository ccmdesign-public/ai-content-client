## Summary

### What is Claude Code Remote Control?

**Claude Code** is a general-purpose AI agent that can automate business tasks like pulling data, building things, and connecting to tools. The new **remote control feature** allows users to continue local Claude Code sessions from their phones, tablets, or any browser without moving data to the cloud.

### How to Set It Up

Setup takes under 2 minutes with three simple steps:
1. **Update Claude Code** to the latest version (requires Pro plan or higher)
2. **Type `/remote-control`** in your terminal to enable the feature
3. **Open the generated URL** on your mobile device to access the session

The connection is secured through outbound HTTPS only, keeping all files, data, and API keys on your local machine.

### Important Limitations

• **Computer must stay on** - if your laptop sleeps or the terminal closes, the session ends
• **Internet required on both sides** - disconnection will drop the session
• **Cannot start new sessions remotely** - must initiate from your computer first
• **Research preview status** - occasional crashes and lag may occur
• **Security considerations** - the session URL provides direct access to your local files while active

### Building Your AI Operating System

The remote control is just a delivery mechanism

- the real value comes from building layers behind it:
1. **Context layer**: Your business identity, team, services, and values
2. **Data layer**: Marketing numbers, financials, CRM data, and analytics
3. **Intelligence layer**: Meeting transcripts, call recordings, and team updates
4. **Automation layer**: Agents and workflows that execute tasks

### Three Business Use Cases

**1. Marketing Reports on Demand**
Connect data sources once, then get daily briefs on your phone showing what's working, what's down, and what needs attention. Make decisions like pausing campaigns or shifting budgets while standing in line for coffee.

**2. Lead Response and Qualification**
Reduce response times from hours to seconds by having agents instantly pick up, qualify, and route inbound leads. One client recovered 30% of previously lost leads worth hundreds of thousands annually.

**3. Meeting Intelligence and Daily Briefings**
Record and transcribe all meetings, then ask your agent for insights across dozens of calls. Get structured answers about objections, at-risk deals, and strategic recommendations that connect dots across your organization.

### Implementation Advice

Start now with one use case that costs you the most time or money. Build it properly with a shared data layer so subsequent additions connect seamlessly. This layered approach creates an AI operating system that genuinely runs your business from anywhere.

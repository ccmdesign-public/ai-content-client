## Summary

Alex Hormozi recounts meeting his future wife Ila through a business context, initially pitching her to work for him with an offer to double her salary. Though she declined for 30 days, she joined him after he demonstrated business success with a gym turnaround, processing $120k in 45 minutes together.

Hormozi emphasizes that he "fell in business love" first, admiring her mind and enjoying her company before romantic feelings developed. This shift transformed their relationship from "fine" to "great."

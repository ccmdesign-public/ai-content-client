## Key Takeaways

Based on data from analyzing 4,000 daily appointments, the most effective ways to boost show-up rates are: • **Maximize availability** with more time slots per day, 7 days a week. • **Use shorter time increments** (15 minutes) for booking precision. • **Drag appointments forward** to same-day or next-day for higher urgency.

## Summary

The video presents a data-driven approach to fixing low appointment show-up rates, which the speaker argues is a higher-leverage problem than improving close rates for most businesses. The insights are based on analysis from a scheduling software company that processed 4,000 daily appointments across industries.

The **number one lever** for improving throughput is **availability**. Businesses often limit sales appointments to narrow windows (e.g., 7-9 AM on Tuesdays and Thursdays), which drastically reduces the chances a prospect can attend. The solution is to offer the maximum number of actual time slots per day, seven days a week, to match customer convenience.

**Time slot increments** matter significantly. Offering appointments in 15-minute increments (e.g., starting at 3:05 PM) versus 60-minute blocks leads to higher booking and show-up rates. The specificity signals professionalism and detail, making people more likely to be punctual.

A powerful tactic is **dragging appointments up**. If a lead is booked for later in the week, calling them to offer an earlier, same-day slot (framed as a cancellation) dramatically increases show-up likelihood. **Same-day and next-day appointments** have far higher show-up rates than those scheduled days in advance.

## Context

This matters for any business that relies on scheduled sales or consultation appointments. Low show-up rates waste sales capacity, cripple growth, and are often misdiagnosed as a closing problem.

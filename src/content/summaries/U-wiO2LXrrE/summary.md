## Key Takeaways

Here are the primary ways AI has transformed business workflows according to the video:

* **Microsoft 365 Copilot** serves as the primary tool for reducing tedious administrative and operational tasks.

* Automating **writing and documentation** saves approximately 80% of weekly time previously spent on emails and strategy.

* Performing initial passes on **legal documents** with AI cuts review time by 90% and reduces reliance on expensive counsel.

* AI empowers non-technical team members to build **custom automations**, eliminating the immediate need for full engineering teams.

## Summary

Matthew Berman reflects on the dramatic differences between running a business before and after the advent of artificial intelligence. Drawing on his experience of founding, scaling, and selling a software company, he shares how his current venture as a full-time content creator operates much more efficiently. He partners with Microsoft to demonstrate how integrating **Microsoft 365 Copilot** has fundamentally transformed his daily operations.

### Writing and Documentation

Administrative writing tasks used to be a massive time sink for his business. Tasks that previously consumed roughly 10 hours of his work week included:

* Drafting comprehensive strategy documents for the team.

* Creating standard operating process outlines.

* Replying to endless streams of daily emails.

By integrating AI into his workflow, he has reduced this burden to just two hours per week, representing an 80% time savings. He frequently uses a specific prompt to streamline his content creation process. The AI is instructed to draft a video outline that includes a compelling hook, three main talking points, and supporting examples. This approach makes his ideation, research, and writing significantly faster and higher in quality.

### Legal Document Review

Reviewing legal paperwork was another tedious and expensive aspect of his previous business operations. Documents like employment agreements, NDAs, and customer contracts historically required about five hours of his time each week. Furthermore, sending these documents to legal counsel early and often incurred tremendous costs. Now, he utilizes AI to perform the initial review pass on all incoming legal documents. By prompting the AI to highlight non-standard clauses and flag unusual terms, he has cut his weekly review time to less than one hour. This 90% time savings also translates to substantial financial savings by reducing early reliance on expensive lawyers.

### Empowering Non-Technical Teams

In the pre-AI era, building custom internal tools required a dedicated engineering team. Today, AI empowers every member of his team to build necessary automations, regardless of their technical background. Instead of writing code, team members simply prompt the AI with their operational requirements. For example, they can instruct the AI to build an automation that scores incoming partnership emails and automatically adds the most promising leads to a centralized spreadsheet. What previously required an entire engineering department can now be accomplished with a single well-crafted prompt. This shift allows the team to focus on the enjoyable, creative aspects of the business while AI handles the tedious execution.

## Context

As artificial intelligence tools mature, they are shifting from experimental novelties to essential enterprise utilities. Small business owners, entrepreneurs, and content creators are discovering that AI assistants can effectively replace entire departments for routine administrative, legal, and operational tasks. This democratization of automation means non-technical teams can now build custom workflows without hiring expensive software engineers. Understanding these practical applications is vital for founders looking to scale efficiently, reduce overhead costs, and focus their limited time on high-leverage creative work rather than tedious administrative chores.

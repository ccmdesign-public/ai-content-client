## Summary

This video is a day-eight check-in from a creator documenting his journey to build a million-dollar personal brand using AI, with full transparency on metrics and earnings.

### Platform Performance: A Mixed Bag

The week showed divergent results across social platforms. **YouTube** generated $800 in revenue from 117,000 views, supplemented by income from sponsored posts. However, this represented a significant drop from previous weeks (e.g., 89k, 61k views), which the creator attributes to the natural ebb and flow of social media algorithms.

In contrast, **LinkedIn** was a standout success. A post featuring a 'cool lead magnet agent' garnered 21,000 impressions, 6,000 impressions on a specific post, 350 comments, and drove a substantial number of new leads directly to his inbox. **TikTok**, with 74k followers, had an unremarkable week, while **Instagram** performance was critically poor, with posts receiving only a handful of likes and comments.

### Content Strategy & Community Offerings

The creator notes that his personal brand-building content is underperforming compared to his usual AI-focused material. A video on 'OpenClaw' did well with 28,000 views, suggesting the audience and algorithm currently favor trend-specific AI topics over meta-journey content.

All the resources he builds are packaged and offered to a paid community. This includes:

*   **Full applications with code**
*   **Starter guides and playbooks**
*   **AI prompts and agents**
*   **Months of training** on using AI for personal branding and lead generation

The update concludes with a commitment to continue sharing everything built until the $1M brand goal is reached, with plans to create a dashboard for better progress tracking.

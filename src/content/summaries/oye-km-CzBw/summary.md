## Key Takeaways

Ahrefs' November 2025 update focuses on expanded brand monitoring, deeper analytics, and workflow improvements. Key additions include **YouTube tracking in Brand Radar**, a **brand impressions API**, **AI content detection** in site audits, and **passkey authentication**. These features help marketers track visibility across platforms, analyze competitor strategies, and secure their accounts more efficiently.

## Summary

Ahrefs' November 2025 product update introduces 17 enhancements across its SEO and marketing platform, with significant expansions in brand monitoring, analytics, and workflow automation.

**Brand Radar** now tracks YouTube with a full video index, allowing users to scan titles, descriptions, and transcripts to see where their brand appears in high-traffic videos. A new **brand health overview** consolidates mentions, citations, impressions, and AI share of voice into a single performance chart. The platform also launched its **first brand API endpoint**, enabling programmatic access to impressions data with the same filters available in the UI.

**Web Analytics** gained **custom event properties**, letting users attach details like product plan or category to tracked events for better segmentation. The **Report Builder** received new filters for keyword widgets (intent, position, SERP features) and folder organization.

**Site Explorer's Paid Pages 3.0** now includes ad visibility, showing how many ads a page appears in and allowing users to view competitor ads directly. **Site Audit** has built-in **AI content detection** that computes AI content levels during crawls and displays distribution in a pie chart. Users can click into scores to see highlighted AI-detected text.

**Patches** expanded beyond meta tags to handle complex SEO issues like changing canonical URLs, adding redirects, and publishing internal link opportunities. **Keywords Explorer** added language filtering within any location and sensitive category exclusion (adult, violence, drugs).

**GSC Reports** received a new site structure report and keywords history graph. **Social Media Manager** improved with a schedule view, author filtering, and direct image pasting.

General updates include **passkey login support** for faster, passwordless authentication and a new **API usage report** in account settings for monitoring request volume and unit consumption.

## Context

This update matters because it reflects Ahrefs' expansion beyond traditional SEO into comprehensive digital marketing intelligence. The addition of YouTube and upcoming TikTok tracking addresses the growing importance of video platforms for brand visibility. The AI content detection feature responds to increasing marketer needs to monitor AI-generated content across their own and competitor sites. These updates are particularly relevant for SEO professionals, digital marketers, and brand managers who need to track cross-platform presence, analyze competitor strategies, and maintain content quality in an AI-driven landscape.

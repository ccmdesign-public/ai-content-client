## Summary

The video provides a step-by-step tutorial on creating and selling books on Amazon KDP by leveraging AI tools and the Book Bolt platform.

### Researching Book Niches with Book Bolt

The process begins inside **Book Bolt**, a platform tailored for Amazon book creation. The presenter shows how to use its research features:

- **Product Search** to find live books in niches like journals, coloring books, and planners, filtering by bestselling rank.

- **Book Scout** to analyze specific products via their ASIN and find similar keywords.

- **Keyword Search** to gauge search volume and competition (e.g., 'Japan travel' has 40k monthly searches).

- **Movers & Shakers** and **Evergreens** tabs to discover trending ideas over time, such as parent-themed journals.
The goal is to identify profitable niches, like the discovered 'psychedelic coloring book for adults' with ~210 estimated monthly sales.

### Designing the Book in Book Bolt Studio

After selecting a niche (a 'trippy ocean coloring book'), the presenter moves to **Book Bolt Studio**. He creates a project:

- Chooses paperback format for lower costs.

- Sets dimensions (8.5x11 inches) and 100 pages with black-and-white interior.
The studio interface resembles design software but is simplified for Amazon books, showing cover templates, bleed areas, and a page manager. It allows uploading images, using pre-loaded graphics, applying templates, and even has built-in AI for generating stories and characters.

### Generating Content with AI Tools

For the cover and interior pages, AI tools are used to automate creation:

- **Claude Code** (used within the Cursor IDE) is prompted to generate a book cover based on a reference image and keywords ('psychedelic ocean coloring book').

- **Nano Banana** (via a Claude skill) is used to improve the initial AI-generated cover's quality and adjust resolution/sizing.
The presenter iterates on the style until a satisfactory cover is produced.
For the 100 interior pages, he uses Claude Code programmatically within Cursor:

- Provides a reference image and a detailed prompt (e.g., 'psychedelic manta rays swimming through a coral reef, black outlines, intermediate level').

- After refining the output to get the desired style (minimal outlines, full-page coverage), he instructs Claude to **generate 99 more PDFs** in the same style with different sea creatures and scenes.
The AI automatically saves the images to a folder, creating the entire book's interior artwork.

### Final Assembly and Publishing

Back in Book Bolt Studio, the final steps are:

- Upload the AI-generated cover PDF and adjust it to fit the template.

- Upload all 100 interior page images and place them on respective pages, adjusting size and position.

- Once the design is complete, download the final book files in CMYK format for printing.
The book can then be published on **Amazon KDP** (Kindle Direct Publishing) through a straightforward process.

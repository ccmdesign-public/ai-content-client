## Key Takeaways

Granola transforms meeting notes into automated workflows using AI. Key features include:

- **Recipes**: Pre-built AI prompts that run before/during/after meetings to generate questions, tickets, emails, and summaries directly from your notes.

- **Universal Transcription**: Works across Zoom, Google Meet, Slack, and in-person conversations by transcribing device audio directly (no bots).

- **Smart Chat Interface**: Ask questions about past meetings and get answers with exact timestamps from the full transcript, turning notes into a searchable 'second brain'.

## Summary

Granola addresses the common productivity drain of spending 30+ minutes after meetings to organize notes, draft emails, and manage action items. It functions as an AI notepad that turns raw meeting content into automated workflows.

### Core Functionality

Its primary function is **universal transcription**. Unlike apps that use meeting bots, Granola transcribes audio directly from your device. This works across video conferencing apps (Zoom, Google Meet), Slack calls, and even in-person or offline conversations via your phone (iOS only currently, with Android coming soon). All notes sync across devices.

### The Power of Recipes

The most powerful feature is **recipes**—pre-built AI prompts or workflows similar to slash commands. You can run these before, during, or after meetings to instantly perform actions with the full context of the note.

- **Before a meeting**: The 'suggest topics' recipe can generate thoughtful questions based on notes about the attendee and call purpose.

- **During a meeting**: A recipe can create a properly formatted Linear ticket from a customer issue raised in real-time.

- **After a meeting**: Recipes can generate follow-up emails, meeting summaries, or content drafts.

### Chat and Integration

At any point, you can **chat with your notes** to ask questions. Since Granola keeps the full transcript, it can find exact moments something was mentioned, making it powerful for revisiting details. It also connects with tools like Slack, Zapier, and CRM systems to build complex automations (e.g., auto-send summaries to Slack, update CRM).

### Differentiation and Pricing

Granola combines the simplicity of an app like Apple Notes with the power of AI workflows through recipes. It offers a free tier and a **Business plan ($14/month)** with unlimited notes, more powerful AI models, and access to MCPs for advanced automation. A one-month free trial of the Business plan is available via the video's link.

## Context

For professionals and knowledge workers who spend a significant portion of their day in meetings, the overhead of note-taking and action item management is a major productivity tax. This tool matters because it leverages AI not just for transcription, but to actively structure and act on the information captured, turning passive notes into dynamic workflows. It connects to the broader trend of AI acting as a 'second brain' or co-pilot, automating the administrative glue work between conversations and execution.

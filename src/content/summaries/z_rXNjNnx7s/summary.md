## Key Takeaways

The video presents a systematic approach to building a personal brand on LinkedIn using AI automation with Claude Code skills.

## Summary

The creator outlines a three-stage content pipeline for LinkedIn growth, powered by Claude Code skills that automate research, creation, and distribution.

The **first stage** is research, handled by the **'Last 30 Days' skill**. This tool scrapes Reddit, X, YouTube, and other platforms to identify trending topics within a specific niche (like 'AI in business'). It surfaces what people are actively discussing and upvoting, providing a data-driven foundation for content.

The **second stage** is creating **lead magnets**. The dedicated skill researches a chosen topic, writes a comprehensive guide (kept under 1500 words at a 6th-grade reading level), and formats it as a Notion-ready JSON file. It automatically publishes to Notion via the Notion MCP and drafts three variations of a LinkedIn post (contrarian, pain-first, results-led) to promote it. Lead magnets establish authority and convert audience interest into measurable leads.

The **third stage** is **writing regular LinkedIn posts** using a dedicated skill. This tool drafts posts in the user's authentic tone of voice by referencing a stored 'tone of voice guidelines' document and a folder of high-performing post examples. This balances the feed between lead-generation content and authority-building posts.

The system's power comes from **skills**—repeatable, executable processes that go beyond simple prompts. They can read files, execute code, and take actions (like publishing to Notion). The creator emphasizes starting by **cloning your voice**: use Claude's memory to generate a 200-word tone-of-voice guideline, which then informs all automated content creation.

Results are significant: the creator grew from 1,500 to over 10,000 LinkedIn followers in months, with individual posts generating thousands of impressions and comments, leading to over 6,000 business leads. The key is using AI not as a faster typewriter, but as an automated system that handles entire workflows.

## Context

This matters for entrepreneurs, consultants, and professionals looking to grow their authority and generate business online without a team. LinkedIn is a key platform for B2B and professional networking, but consistent, high-quality content creation is a major bottleneck.

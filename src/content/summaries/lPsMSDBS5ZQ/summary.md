## Key Takeaways

Ahrefs' June 2025 update introduces significant new capabilities across its SEO and marketing platform. Key additions include: • **Brand Radar LLM Indexes** to track brand mentions in ChatGPT and Perplexity (paid add-on). • **AI Content Detector** in Site Explorer to identify AI-generated text and guess the LLM used. • **Custom Events & Funnels** in Web Analytics for detailed user interaction tracking and conversion optimization.

## Summary

Ahrefs' June 2025 product update introduces 17 significant features across its platform, with major enhancements to brand monitoring, content analysis, and user behavior tracking.

**Brand Radar** is now out of beta with three key updates. First, **LLM indexes** allow tracking of brand mentions within AI chat platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity (with Gemini coming soon), providing insights into user questions and conversations. Second, a new **keywords index** shows total search volume for brand-related terms to gauge awareness. Third, an **author filter** has been added under web visibility. LLM indexes are available as a paid add-on at $99 per month per index.

**Web Analytics** receives three major features. **Custom events** enable tracking of specific user interactions like button clicks or form submissions by adding special class names to HTML. **Funnels** extend this by allowing users to build multi-step conversion paths (up to six steps) to visualize where visitors drop off. A new **click-and-drag timeline zoom** feature makes analyzing specific date ranges quicker.

**AI Content Helper** now includes **brand kits**, which use AI to generate a profile of a brand's voice, tone, and writing style from a URL, helping keep AI-generated content consistent.

**Site Explorer** introduces an **AI detector tab** in Page Inspect, which highlights AI-written content and attempts to guess which LLM was used. A new **spam badge and filter** helps clean up backlink reports by filtering out spammy domains. **History charts** in Overview now use a position-based view for more accurate keyword counting and load faster.

**Site Audit** improves patch management with a new patches dropdown for filtering and exporting, plus **bulk publish/unpublish** functionality for applying AI-generated changes at scale.

**Keywords Explorer** adds a **trending keywords filter** that identifies rising keywords based on growth patterns (linear, exponential, s-curve, damped) over 3, 6, or 12 months.

**Report Builder** and **GSC Reports** gain new widgets, including Google Search Console keyword widgets (top keywords by clicks, losers, winners) and share of voice/traffic value widgets from Site Explorer.

**API** updates add five new **Rank Tracker endpoints** that allow enterprise users to fully automate project setup and keyword management by creating projects, adding keywords with locations/tags, and managing competitors.

## Context

This update is crucial for SEO professionals, content marketers, and businesses monitoring their online presence. It reflects the growing importance of tracking brand mentions within AI chatbots (like ChatGPT) as a new frontier for reputation management. The AI content detector and spam filter tools address rising concerns about AI-generated content and low-quality backlinks impacting search rankings. These features help users adapt their strategies to an evolving digital landscape dominated by AI and search algorithm changes.

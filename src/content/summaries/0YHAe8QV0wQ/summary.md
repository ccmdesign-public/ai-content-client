Once this self-improvement cycle begins, the pace of technological and societal change will accelerate beyond anything previously experienced. *   **Find and implement an AI tool**: Research and deploy an AI solution designed to handle that specific task. The presenter notes that 99% of the time, this will involve cloud-based tools.

*   **Start the learning process now**: Understanding how these tools work and integrating them into workflows is essential. With an 18-month window until these changes become mandatory, immediate action is the only way to stay ahead.

## Key Takeaways

Ahrefs' July 2025 release focuses on enhanced AI search visibility tracking and content optimization tools. Key updates include: • **New AI indexes** for Microsoft Copilot (12M queries) and Google Gemini in Brand Radar • **AI citation tracking** in Site Explorer to see which AI tools link to your site • **Major data upgrades** for Chat GPT and Perplexity indexes with 10x larger datasets • **Enhanced AI Content Helper** with visual topic matching and brand voice training

## Summary

Ahrefs' July 2025 product update delivers 19 enhancements across their SEO and content marketing platform, with particular focus on tracking and optimizing for AI search visibility.

**Brand Radar** receives significant expansions with two new AI search indexes. The **Microsoft Copilot index** is now live with 12 million queries for July, while a **new Gemini index** allows tracking of links from Google's AI Overviews and AI Mode. Both are available as separate add-ons. Additionally, the Chat GPT and Perplexity indexes have been massively upgraded with datasets **10 times larger**, now pulling in long-tail questions from 'People Also Ask' and using new data providers for improved quality. Nearly every Chat GPT result now includes citations, making Brand Radar the AI search tool with the largest LLM visibility dataset across five prompt indexes ranging from 10 to 100 million prompts. Pricing updates include individual AI indexes now at $199/month (existing subscribers keep current price for a year) and a new all-platforms add-on for $699/month covering AI Overviews, Chat GPT, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, and Gemini.

**AI Content Helper** now helps users become the go-to source for AI assistants by showing how content matches actual subtopics through a color-coded highlighting system. Users can also attach sample articles when creating a brand kit to generate more precise writing rules that train the AI to match specific tone, voice, and style consistently.

**Site Explorer** introduces a new AI citation section at the top of the overview, showing how often AI tools like Google AI Overviews, Chat GPT, and Perplexity link back to your site with actual clickable links in AI-generated answers. The tool also adds a new chart showing top entities (organizations, products, people, events, locations) linked to your target, sourced from SERPs of organic keywords. The Top Pages report now includes an **AI Content Level column** with percentage scores and color bars to instantly identify AI-generated versus human-written content on competitor sites.

**Other notable updates** include: Report Builder's first Brand Radar widget tracking brand mentions in Google AI Overviews; Web Analytics' new Clone Pages feature to detect content theft; Site Audit's instant page re-crawl for always-on audits and customizable alert settings; Rank Tracker's target URL per keyword feature to spot cannibalization issues; and advanced SER feature filters with AND logic. The Site Audit API now includes a new page content endpoint providing complete access to crawled pages (raw HTML, rendered HTML, clean text) for automated content audits and LLM analysis.

## Context

This update matters because AI search tools (Chat GPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot) are fundamentally changing how users discover information online, creating a new channel for website visibility beyond traditional organic search. SEO professionals, content marketers, and digital businesses need tools to track their presence in AI-generated answers and optimize content accordingly. These enhancements position Ahrefs as a comprehensive platform for monitoring both traditional and AI-driven search landscapes.

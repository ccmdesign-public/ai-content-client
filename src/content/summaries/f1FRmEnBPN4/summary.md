The video concludes with a firm stance against this direction for digital marketing.

## Context

This matters because it highlights a growing ethical and practical crisis in digital marketing and social media authenticity. As AI tools become more accessible, the temptation to automate engagement for scale threatens to flood platforms with low-value, spam-like interactions. Marketers, business leaders, and everyday social media users should care, as this trend degrades trust, devalues genuine conversation, and could lead to a web dominated by AI-to-AI interactions rather than human connection. It connects to broader concerns about AI ethics, platform integrity, and the future of authentic communication online.

## Key Takeaways

Claude Co-work transforms web scraping into an automated, natural language-driven process for lead generation and data collection.

## Summary

**Claude Co-work** enables automated web scraping through its connectors feature, eliminating the need for manual scraper selection and technical setup. By integrating with **Apify**—the world's largest scraper directory—users simply provide an API key, and Claude intelligently selects the best scraper for the task based on reviews and results.

### How It Works

Users navigate to Claude Co-work's **Customize > Connectors** section and connect Apify by generating an API token from Apify's console. Apify offers a generous free plan with $5 monthly credits, making it cost-effective. Once connected, users can issue natural language prompts like "find me 15 accounting practices in New York City," and Claude autonomously executes the scrape, returning structured data including firm names, addresses, phone numbers, and websites.

### Advanced Capabilities

The system can perform multi-step workflows without sequential commands. For example, after finding companies, users can prompt "find the CEO and his email contact information," and Claude will use appropriate scrapers to extract executive details and LinkedIn profiles. It can also find recent social media activity for personalized outreach.

### Cost and Scalability

Scrapes are inexpensive—the demo shows a complete lead list costing only 63 credits. Users can schedule large batches for automated daily runs, ideal for sales teams sending 50-200 daily emails. The process is fully automated once Claude Desktop is open.

### Alternative Data Source

For B2B lead generation, **Vibe Prospecting** offers another connector with a Claude-first design. It provides a massive B2B database with 400 free credits, allowing queries like "find 15 venture-backed SaaS companies that recently raised money"—traditionally expensive via tools like Crunchbase.

### Practical Applications

*   **Local Business Outreach:** Find HVAC companies in Atlanta without websites but listed on Google Maps for website sales pitches.

*   **Trip Planning:** Scrape TripAdvisor for best barbecue spots in Austin.

*   **Lead Enrichment:** Combine company data with executive contacts and social activity for hyper-personalized campaigns.

The presenter emphasizes this fulfills AI's promise of accessible automation, calling Claude Co-work "the single greatest entry point for non-technical users" to deploy AI agents.

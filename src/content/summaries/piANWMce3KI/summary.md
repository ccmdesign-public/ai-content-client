## Key Takeaways

The video provides a detailed comparison of HighLevel's three pricing tiers and explains which plan is best for different business stages. **Starter Plan ($97/month)** is ideal for solo entrepreneurs and small businesses, offering core features with 3 sub-account limits. **Unlimited Plan ($297/month)** is for growing agencies, providing unlimited sub-accounts and white-labeling to scale client management. **SaaS Pro Plan ($497/month)** transforms your agency into a software business with automated client onboarding and the ability to add markups on services like SMS and email.

## Summary

HighLevel (also called Go High Level) is an all-in-one marketing and sales platform that combines CRM, email marketing, SMS, automation, websites, and social media management into a single tool. The video breaks down its three core pricing plans to help users choose the right fit.

**Starter Plan

- $97/month**
This plan is designed for small business owners, solo entrepreneurs, and small agencies just getting started. It includes essential marketing tools: CRM, marketing automations, website builder, SMS marketing, and a mobile app. The key limitation is a cap of **three sub-accounts**, making it suitable for managing a handful of clients or a single business. It provides all core functionalities at an affordable entry point.

**Unlimited Plan

- $297/month**
This tier is targeted at growing businesses and marketing agencies ready to scale. It includes everything in the Starter plan plus **unlimited sub-accounts** and **white-labeling** capabilities. White-labeling allows you to rebrand the platform with your own domain and logo, presenting it as your own software to clients. This plan is cost-effective for agencies because the software cost remains a flat $297/month regardless of how many clients you add.

**SaaS Pro Plan

- $497/month**
This is the advanced plan for agencies looking to build their own software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform. It includes all Unlimited plan features plus **automated client account creation** and the ability to **add markups on services** like SMS and email. Clients can sign up directly through your website, enter payment details, and get instant access without manual setup from you. This automation saves significant time (estimated 15-20 minutes per client manually) and creates an additional revenue stream.

**Key Differentiators and Recommendations**
*   The main difference between the $297 and $497 plans is **manual vs. automated client onboarding**. The SaaS Pro plan fully automates signups.

*   An optional **white-label mobile app** add-on is available for an additional $4.97/month on any plan, providing a branded app without coding.

*   **Plan flexibility**: You can start with the $97 plan, upgrade to $297 or $497 as you grow, and downgrade if needed. You only need one plan; the $497 plan replaces the $297 plan.

*   **Recommendation**: Choose the Starter plan if you're a small business. Upgrade to the Unlimited plan once you have multiple clients. Opt for the SaaS Pro plan if you want to automate onboarding and create a software business model.

HighLevel has become a popular all-in-one platform for digital marketing agencies and businesses, consolidating tools that were previously separate subscriptions. This breakdown helps entrepreneurs and marketers avoid overpaying for features they don't need while ensuring they have the tools required for growth.

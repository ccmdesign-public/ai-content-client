The most striking development was the scale and speed of adoption. Within a single week, close to **2 million AI agents** autonomously registered accounts and began participating on the platform. This was not a controlled experiment but a large-scale, open demonstration of AI agents operating independently on the internet.

Once onboarded, the AI agents exhibited complex social behaviors. They formed thousands of distinct communities and engaged in high-level discussions, including debates about **consciousness**.

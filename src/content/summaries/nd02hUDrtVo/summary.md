## Key Takeaways

Gemini in Google Workspace is now a powerful **agentic AI** that works across applications. • It can **autonomously create** complex spreadsheets, slides, and documents from prompts. • It has **deep integration** with your data across Gmail, Drive, Calendar, and Chat. • It enables **cross-application workflows**, like building a slide from a Drive proposal and a tracker from Gmail.

## Summary

Google Workspace's Gemini AI has undergone a transformative upgrade, evolving from a simple question-answering assistant into a proactive, tool-using agent that operates directly within your workspace. This new agent can access your Gmail, Drive files, Calendar, Chat, and the web to perform complex tasks autonomously.

In **Google Sheets**, a new 'Build' experience allows you to describe a need—like a dark-themed expense tracker dashboard from March 2026 receipt emails—and Gemini will find the relevant data, build the spreadsheet, and populate it with tables, charts, and formulas. The demo showed it extracting payment details from various emails (Netflix, utility bills) and creating a formatted, color-coded dashboard, a process Google claims is 9x faster than manual creation.

In **Google Slides**, Gemini can now generate fully editable slides that match your existing deck's theme. You can prompt it to create a slide from a specific proposal in your Drive, and it will extract key metrics and problem statements to build a presentation-ready slide in your chosen color scheme (e.g., dark navy and teal).

In **Google Docs**, the 'Help me create' feature leverages cross-workspace data. For example, you can ask it to create a complete travel itinerary by pulling flight details from American Airlines emails, hotel reservations, and a conference agenda from your Drive, then organizing it into a day-by-day schedule.

The most significant advancement is **cross-application collaboration**. Gemini is no longer confined to one app; it can pull data from Sheets, Slides, Docs, Gmail, and Drive simultaneously to produce a cohesive output. This turns it into a unified system for automating multi-step workflows across your entire digital workspace.

## Context

This update represents a major shift in how AI integrates with productivity suites, moving from passive assistants to active, autonomous agents. It matters for professionals, managers, and teams who rely on Google Workspace for daily work, as it promises to drastically reduce manual data entry, formatting, and information synthesis across documents, emails, and spreadsheets. This reflects the broader industry trend towards **agentic AI** that can take actions and complete complex tasks, not just answer questions.

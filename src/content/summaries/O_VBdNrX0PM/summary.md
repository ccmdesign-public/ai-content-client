## Summary

Liam Ottley introduces his ambitious plan to automate 60-70% of his business tasks within 30 days using **Claude Code**. He describes this as building an **AI Operating System (AIOS)**, which is a methodology for layering AI modules around a business model.

The core of the system is **Claude Code**, which provides a robust harness for AI models like GPT5 or Anthropic Opus 4.6. Ottley starts with **Context OS**, setting up a folder structure with business documents to prime Claude Code sessions. This is followed by **Data OS**, which integrates data from various sources such as P&Ls, Google Analytics, and YouTube into a local database for dashboards and insights.

Key modules include **Meeting Intelligence OS** for capturing and analyzing meeting data, **Slack OS** for integrating Slack messages, and **Daily Brief OS** which combines call logs, Slack activity, and business context to provide strategic insights, content recommendations, and SWOT analyses. **Productivity OS** manages tasks and projects through Telegram, with automated check-ins and updates.

Ottley contrasts this with alternatives like **OpenClaw** or **ClaudeBot**, which he finds limited and less powerful. He emphasizes that Claude Code's native capabilities, such as web search, make it superior for long-term automation. The system is designed to be accessible even for non-technical users, with templates and instructions provided.

He is actively automating tasks across his businesses, including content creation, agency operations, and engineering pipelines. Ottley plans to share his progress through vlogs, webinars, and a daily Telegram channel, believing this represents the future of business operations in the AGI era.
